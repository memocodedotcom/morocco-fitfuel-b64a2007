# Visual 3.0: Avant-Garde Architectural Luxury

## Objective
To transform NutriMaroc from a rigid, "robotic" interface into a fluid, world-class luxury experience. We will replace clinical grids with **Cinematic Continuity**, **Prestige Typography**, and **Asymmetric Balance**.

### 1. Visual Strategy: "Human-Centric Precision"
**Challenge**: Current UI feels "robotic" (all-caps, sharp boxes, cold colors).  
**Solution**: 
*   **Typography**: Introduce a refined Serif (e.g., *Playfair Display*) for large emotional statements. Keep the geometric Sans for functional data.
*   **Palette**: Soften Obsidian with subtle "Titanium and Glass" effects.
*   **Hierarchy**: Use overlapping containers and asymmetric spacing to break the "random box" feel.

### 2. Component Refinement
*   **Hero 3.0**: Cinematic layers with a high-contrast serif headline "LA PERFORMANCE" that feels like a magazine cover.
*   **Product & Story Grids**: Asymmetric layout where products feel curated rather than just listed.
*   **Refined Spacing**: Use `max-w-7xl` or `max-w-6xl` consistently but with uneven horizontal margins for a more "intentional" aesthetic.

**Status**: Ready for implementation.
